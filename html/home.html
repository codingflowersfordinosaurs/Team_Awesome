Be your own bartender

We are here to bring basic mixology to the masses. Many classic drinks are simple combinations of easy to acquire ingredients. Our logic engine lets you confidently select new options among tried and true classics.

About

We start with a base spirit (vodka, gin, rum, bourbon, etc), and then we go from there. We connect you to videos for all of the recipes, no matter how simple.

Mix with confidence.

Recipes

The heart of our project: Tasty classics with ingredients you should be able to acquire easily. Many of the best drinks only require 2-5 ingredients. We'll tell you what you need and how to use it.

Your Home Bar

Setting up a home bar? We got you. We will help you figure our what you need to entertain, be able to make several classic drinks at home while not breaking the bank.

Bars

Want to get fancy? Thinking of elaborate specialty drinks? Leave that to the professionals. We will help you find the best cocktail bars near you. No need to spend your hard earned cash on specialty ingredients that you will buy once, not get right, and will let sit in your cupboard and mock you.

Team

We are values- and culture-driven. We work with honesty, transparency, and "no ego".
